The Four Quadrants

Settled + Expansive

The Confident

Builds boldly from certainty

You know what you believe and you're ready to act on it. The future is an opportunity, not a threat. You trust that abundance is possible and you're already building toward it. Your certainty gives others permission to move.

You might be: Confident, Assured, or Bold

Settled + Protective

The Grounded

Guards what matters from a place of knowing

You know what's worth protecting and you don't need to explain it. Not everything that's old is outdated. Not everything that's new is progress. You hold the line so others can find their footing.

You might be: Steady, Grounded, or Anchored

Seeking + Expansive

The Curious

Explores without needing answers first

You don't need to know where you're going to start moving. The unknown is interesting, not frightening. You ask questions others haven't thought to ask and you're comfortable living in the space before answers arrive.

You might be: Adaptive, Curious, or Open

Seeking + Protective

The Careful

Protects while still discovering

You haven't figured it all out yet, and that's exactly why you're cautious. You've seen what happens when people move fast without understanding the stakes. You ask "what could go wrong?" because someone has to.

You might be: Careful, Cautious, or Measured

Builder or Witness?

Within each quadrant, some people shape the world (Builders) while others perceive it (Witnesses). Builders act, create, and make change. Witnesses notice, reflect, and hold space. Neither is better. Both are needed.
