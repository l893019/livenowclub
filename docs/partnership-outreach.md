```

I am submitting a free, comprehensive cancer guide for potential inclusion in your patient resource library.

**Resource Details:**
- Title: "Expecting the Unexpected: Lou's Guide to Cancer"
- Author: Louise Ireland (Stage IV cervical cancer survivor)
- Format: Web-based + PDF download
- Cost: Free
- URL: https://livenowclub.com/navigate/cancer

**Content Overview:**
The guide provides practical, survivor-written advice for:
- Newly diagnosed patients preparing for treatment
- Patients currently undergoing chemotherapy and/or radiation
- Caregivers and family members
- Individuals in survivorship

**Why This is Valuable:**
Unlike medical handouts, this guide fills the gap between "you have cancer" and "here's your treatment plan." It covers the practical, emotional, and logistical realities that patients need to know but often aren't told:
- How to set up a care team and communication system
- What supplies actually help during treatment
- How to prepare your home for healing
- What caregivers should (and shouldn't) do
- Mental health resources and coping strategies

The guide is based on my personal experience navigating both Stage III and Stage IV diagnoses, combined with research and consultation with other cancer survivors.

**Request:**

1. Adding this to your patient resource portal?
```
