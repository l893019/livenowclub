## Pattern Notes

Looking at these 4, the generation logic is clear:

**Noun determines:**
- What they DO (build, shape, observe, witness)
- Their relationship to action/agency
- Their superpower flavor

**Adjective determines:**
- HOW they do it (confidently, carefully, curiously, steadily)
- Their orientation toward certainty and change
- Their blind spot flavor

**Combinations create natural tensions:**
- Curious Architect's ally is an observer (they need grounding)
- Steady Witness's tension is with builders (they challenge stillness)
- Bold Shaper needs careful questioners (to check momentum)
- Careful Observer needs explorers (to break analysis paralysis)

This should scale to all 84.
