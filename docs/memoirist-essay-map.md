## CRITICAL: Miscategorization Warnings

**"Fixing the Unfixable" is NOT primarily grief.**
It's foundational philosophy. The V1 design put it in "Grief & Loss" - WRONG. It belongs in Start Here or as a core philosophical piece.

**"Life is not empty" is NOT just about recovery.**
It's also about AI, languages, creation, and building. Could live in Wonder as easily as Recovery.

**"Soul.md" is NOT just about AI.**
It's deeply about meaning, productivity, identity, and disability. Core philosophy essay that happens to discuss AI.

**The Cancer Meditations are a SERIES.**
They should never be separated or randomly distributed. Present together, in order.
