**the forest path**

God is in the light,  
but holier,  
maybe,  
in the green hush  
where it filters down  
dappled through   
veined leaves of  
stained glass.

No bells here,  
only birds.  
A single crow calls  
one long note,  
and the forest answers  
by continuing to be.

Somewhere behind you,  
your old life sighs  
and sits down.  
The leaves cover it softly  
like a prayer  
that doesn’t need  
to be answered.
